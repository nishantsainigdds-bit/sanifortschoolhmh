Playgroup

Age: 2–3 Years 3 Hours/Day Max 15 Kids/Class

Our Playgroup program is a child's first step into the world of learning. Designed for toddlers aged 2–3 years, this program focuses on making the transition from home to school smooth, joyful, and exciting.

Through sensory play, music, movement, and social interaction, children develop confidence, communication skills, and a love for learning in a warm, nurturing environment.

Nursery

Age: 3–4 Years 4 Hours/Day Max 18 Kids/Class

The Nursery program builds on the foundation of Playgroup, introducing structured learning through play. Children aged 3–4 years begin exploring letters, numbers, shapes, and colors in a fun, engaging way.

Our Montessori-inspired approach encourages independence, problem-solving, and creativity while developing essential pre-reading and pre-writing skills.

Kindergarten

Age: 4–6 Years 5 Hours/Day Max 20 Kids/Class

Our Kindergarten program prepares children for formal schooling with a comprehensive curriculum covering literacy, numeracy, science, social studies, and the arts. Children develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills.

By the end of Kindergarten, children are confident readers, writers, and thinkers — fully prepared for Grade 1 and beyond.

Our Approach

How We Teach & Learn

🎮

Play-Based Learning

Children learn best through play. Our curriculum integrates structured and free play to develop all areas of development.

🧠

Multiple Intelligences

We recognize that every child is unique. Our activities cater to visual, auditory, kinesthetic, and other learning styles.

🌍

Theme-Based Units

Monthly themes connect learning across subjects, making education meaningful and contextual for young learners.

📊

Continuous Assessment

Regular observation-based assessments track each child's progress without the stress of formal exams.
