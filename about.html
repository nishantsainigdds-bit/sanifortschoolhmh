Our Story

Where Every Child Shines Bright

Founded in 2014, Sanifort Pre-School was born from a simple belief — every child deserves a joyful, safe, and enriching start to their educational journey.

Located in the heart of Hanumangarh, we have grown into a trusted institution serving hundreds of families. Our approach combines the best of modern early childhood education with the warmth of a nurturing home environment.

We believe that the first six years of a child's life are the most critical for brain development. That's why every activity, every interaction, and every moment at Sanifort is thoughtfully designed to spark curiosity, build confidence, and foster a lifelong love of learning.
